About

Born and raised in India. From a young age, I always knew I had a passion for technology, but life has a funny way of guiding us through unexpected paths. I initially pursued a Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering, navigating the intricacies of structures and materials. But deep down, I always felt the pull towards something more aligned with my interests—computers and data.

Driven by this inner calling, I took a leap and started learning programming languages and diving into the world of problem-solving. This journey led me to a role as a Systems Engineer at a renowned service-based MNC, where I spent 1.5 years refining my skills. During this time, I tackled projects that ranged from optimizing performance models to enhancing data migration processes, constantly pushing the boundaries of what was possible.

But I wasn’t satisfied with just scratching the surface. I wanted to dive deeper into the ocean of data science. That’s when I decided to pack my bags and head to the USA to pursue a Master’s degree in Data Science. This move was a game-changer. I immersed myself in cutting-edge technologies and tools, took on challenging academic projects, and honed my expertise in machine learning. The culmination of my efforts was a successful capstone project that showcased my ability to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world problems.

Throughout this journey, I have consistently sought to enhance my understanding and application of data science, machine learning, and AI. From mastering statistical models to exploring deep learning, my dedication has been unwavering. But more importantly, I’ve learned the value of following one’s passion.

I’m always open to connecting with like-minded individuals who share a love for data, technology, and innovation. Whether you’re looking to collaborate, exchange ideas, or simply chat about the latest trends in the tech world, I’d love to hear from you. Let’s explore the endless possibilities that data holds together!
